Liangqu Long

My research interests lie in machine learning (deep learning especially) and its application to a range of perception problems in the fields of artificial intelligence, such as computer vision, robotics, audio recognition, and text processing. I am particularly interested in developing algorithms for automatically learning feature representations from unlabeled data. I am also interested in computer technology such as operating system, driver and intelligent hardware.
